/* Privacy Policy for Grocery Ordering Application */

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction
Grocery Ordering Application ("App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect
We collect information you provide:
- Login credentials (UserId)
- Order information (items ordered, delivery address)
- Payment status (Cash on Delivery)

Auto-collected information:
- Device type and OS version
- App usage statistics
- API request logs

We do NOT collect:
- Password (only hash is stored)
- Credit card information (Cash on Delivery only)
- Location data
- Contacts or personal files

3. How We Use Information
- Authenticate users
- Process and manage orders
- Provide customer support
- Improve app functionality
- Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Security
- HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
- Passwords hashed using industry-standard algorithms
- JWT tokens with expiration
- No storage of sensitive payment data
- Regular security audits

5. User Rights
Users have the right to:
- Access their personal data
- Request data deletion
- Opt-out of non-essential communications

To exercise these rights, contact: privacy@groceryapp.com

6. Third-Party Services
The App uses:
- Microsoft Azure (cloud hosting) - see Azure Privacy Policy
- Google Play Store (distribution) - see Google Privacy Policy
- Authentication via JWT tokens

7. Data Retention
- Order history: 2 years
- User accounts: Until deletion requested
- Authentication tokens: 8 hours
- Log files: 90 days

8. Children's Privacy
The App is not intended for users under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy
We may update this policy. Users will be notified of significant changes.

10. Contact Us
For privacy questions:
Email: privacy@groceryapp.com
Address: Grocery App Support Team

Compliance:
- GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
- CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
- India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act
